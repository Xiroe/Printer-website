HP LaserJet 1320 overview:

The HP LaserJet 1320 printer features simple USB 2.0-compatible and parallel port connectivity. Count on HP reliability and performance. Featuring a 133 MHz processor and 16 MB of RAM (expandable up to 144 MB of RAM), the printer meets your business needs and gracefully handles complex documents. Look sharp.

HP LaserJet P2015 overview:

The HP LaserJet P2015 Printer series features a 400 MHz processor and 32 MB standard memory, expandable to 288 MB. Now you can handle large, graphics-intensive files and get a quick return to the print job's application, as well as increased uptime.

HP LaserJet P2035 overview:

The LaserJet P2035 Printer from HP features a fast 30ppm print speed and a 600x600 dpi resolution with HP's Resolution Enhancement technology (REt). ... The printer is equipped with a 250-sheet input tray and a 50-sheet multipurpose tray which accommodates a wide range of print media.

HP LaserJet P2055 overview:

In addition to its radical simplicity, the HP LaserJet P2055 Printer series also enables high productivity through fast speeds, easy supplies and device manageability, and automatic two sided printing. Safeguard your business with security capabilities that help protect devices and critical information on your network.
